Flappy McFlapface

As I explore game design more, I decided to use a tutorial to build a clone for Flappy Bird on Unity. You can follow the same tutorial here.

Once I'd followed the tutorial, I extended it further with the following enhancements:

  • Added clouds in the background, that do not collide and spawn at random sizes and speeds to create an illusion of depth to the graphics
  • Increased the spawn rate of the pipes for every multiple of 5 the score reaches, to ramp up the difficult the further you go
  • Keep track of the highest score achieved to give you a target to beat
  • Added sounds effects for passing a pipe, the increase after every 5 score and game over
  • Added a start screen so you can start when you are ready rather than going straight into it
  • Added a settings page that allows you to turn off the sound effects
