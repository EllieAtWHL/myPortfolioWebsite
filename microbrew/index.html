Microbrew

Microbrew is a medium-weight worker placement / puzzle game hybrid for two players.

Kickstarted by One Free Elephant in May 2019.

You can print and play your own copy or buy from any of the retailers. I started working on this digital version as I enjoyed the game but found it fiddly, so wondered how a digital version might work.
