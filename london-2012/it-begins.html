    It Begins...

    People often ask me how I got involved in the Olympic Ceremonies. In all honesty, I don't remember the exact moment, but this is what I do know.

    Even before applying, I was obsessed with the Olympics. I spent way too much time during maternity leave taking to the internet for any leads on how to get tickets. I had already been in communication with a group of Twitter users, that I would become part of, now known as "the 2012 Tweeps". A year before the ceremony, and before I even applied, I posted how excited I was - a whole year out!

    One year to go until the opening ceremony, I know some people are already bored of all the hype, but I don't think I ever will.

    Posted by Eleanor Matthewman on Wednesday, 27 July 2011

    I suspect there was a link on one of the many email newsletters that I'd signed up to, looking for volunteers for the ceremonies, and on seeing the advert, I posted on Facebook how I was "tempted to apply".

    So tempted to apply!

    Posted by Eleanor Matthewman on Tuesday, 16 August 2011

    Apply I did, and that is where my official journey to be part of the Olympic Games began...
