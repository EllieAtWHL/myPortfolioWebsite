Regicide

Regicide is a challenging, card game based on a standard 52 card deck.

Kickstarted by Badgers From Mars in May 2020.

You can buy your own copy or learn to play. I hope you learn to love it as much as I do!

Note

Still a work in progress, known bugs/missing features include:

  • Jester not implemented - special solo rules to use for it
  • Not mobile-friendly
  • Add ability to undo card selection
